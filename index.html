💡 Water Conservation Tips

Irrigation Methods

Drip Irrigation: Delivers water directly to plant roots, reducing evaporation by up to 50%.

Sprinkler Systems: More efficient than flooding, saves about 30% water.

Traditional Flooding: Least efficient, loses much water to evaporation.

Rainwater Harvesting

Collecting rainwater reduces dependency on groundwater and helps recharge aquifers naturally.

Vegetation & Trees

Trees improve groundwater recharge by allowing water to percolate slowly into the soil.

Household Tips

• Fix leaky taps - saves up to 20 liters/day

• Take shorter showers - saves 10 liters/minute

• Use full loads in washing machines

• Install water-efficient fixtures
